About

Ordinary is a fairly new travel agency in Erbil that started in 2024. It was founded by two young friends who have 4 years of experience in tourism guidance and visa consultancy. We believe in being honest and providing quality service. That's how we run our business.

Our Mission

Our mission is to establish a new standard of transparency in Kurdistan's travel industry. We provide clients with accurate visa assessments, honest consultancy, and fair pricing from the start. We prioritize long-term client relationships over short-term profits, ensuring that every recommendation we make serves our clients' best interests, even when that means declining business.
